Advanced AI R&D Training Program for Sports Game Applications

Training Program 2022.09 – 2022.12

This project was part of an advanced AI R&D training program focused on sports game applications. Through this experience, I developed practical understanding of AI problem solving in interactive environments and strengthened my ability to apply AI techniques to real application scenarios.

Project Overview

This program aimed to foster advanced AI capabilities for sports game applications by providing hands-on training in practical AI development. It offered an opportunity to understand how AI techniques could be applied to interactive and dynamic problem settings.

Through this experience, I strengthened my ability to approach application-oriented AI tasks, especially in environments where perception, decision-making, and interaction needed to be considered together.

What I Learned

  • Gained hands-on experience in practical AI model development
  • Learned how AI can be applied to interactive sports game environments
  • Improved understanding of problem-solving workflows in application-driven AI tasks
  • Expanded experience in connecting AI theory with implementation

Outcome

This program broadened my perspective on applied AI and helped me build a stronger foundation for later projects in computer vision and deep learning. It was especially meaningful as an early experience in translating AI concepts into practical development tasks.
