About

I am a Vision AI researcher and engineer with experience across both industrial applications and academic research. My work combines practical development in 2D object detection with research experience in 3D point cloud processing and human landmark detection.

Currently, I work as a Senior AI Researcher in the Physical AI Team at HD Korea Shipbuilding & Offshore Engineering, where I contribute to real-world Vision AI development for industrial environments. My interests lie in building reliable computer vision systems that are not only technically strong, but also practical for deployment.

In parallel, my academic background has focused on 3D human understanding, including anthropometric landmark detection, posture-robust estimation, and learning-based analysis of human scan data. By combining 2D and 3D perspectives, I aim to develop Vision AI systems that can adapt to diverse data modalities and real application scenarios.
